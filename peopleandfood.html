People

The Philippines is a country with great people called Filipinos. A couple of years ago, the Philippines were considered one of the World’s Friendliest Islands. The Filipinos love to celebrate whether on the street, beach, city or the mountains. This lifestyle offers a guarantee to any person willing on visiting the Philippines. Every year they hold a festival showing dance and diverse types of Filipino culture. Just like, festival of Ati-Atihan. This festival is to honour the Santo Niño in several towns of the province of Aklan, Panay Island and is held annually in January. Filipino people’s hospitality, strength, warmth, and indomitable spirit are their best qualities.

Filipino Food

The food of the Philippines brings the family together. Pinoy flavours draw inspiration from different international cuisines, but it has evolved through the decades. No gathering would be complete without Lechon, a roasted pig. The skin of it is crispy with secret herbs and spices and it melts in your mouth after the bite. Filipino dishes are labeled as unique. Philippines' rich cultural history and the different flavours is what make up the Filipino cuisine.
